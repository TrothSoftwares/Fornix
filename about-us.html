About Us

Established in the year 2014, FORNIX HEALTH CARE is a leading pharmaceutical company from Punjab dealing in various specialty medicines and also a well-known name in pharmaceutical industry.

We have earned a reputation in the market owing to superior quality medicines. The stupendous success, which the company has achieved in such short span, reflects its strong commitment. FORNIX today is an established name in Pharmaceutical industry and we are constantly growing.
