About Us

EEstablished in the year 2014, FORNIX HEALTH CARE is a leading pharmaceutical company from Punjab dealing in various specialty medicines and also a well-known name in pharmaceutical industry.

We have earned a reputation in the market owing to superior quality medicines. The stupendous success, which the company has achieved in such short span, reflects its strong commitment. FORNIX today is an established name in Pharmaceutical industry and we are constantly growing.

At FORNIX HEALTH CARE, we continually strive to be the best at what we do by providing excellent services in the pharmaceutical industry.

We develop a synergy and a bond of trust through the vast range of best and advanced pharmaceutical products for life saving. We are dedicated people who work together to produce and put efforts to save lives.
